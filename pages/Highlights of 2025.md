<h3>2025: A Year of Growth, Connection, and Excellence at Tax Academy</h3>
<p><strong>January</strong> launched our e-learning platform for Level 1 Income
Tax, GST, and International Tax with revision workshops, making quality
tax education accessible across borders.</p>
<p><strong>February</strong> strengthened our collaboration with WU Transfer
Pricing Center, welcoming the 10th cohort of the WU-TA Advanced Transfer
Pricing Programme and celebrating ten years of partnership.</p>
<p><strong>March</strong> formalised our relationship with the International
Fiscal Association through an MOU signing, enhancing Singapore's voice
in global tax conversations.</p>
<p><strong>April</strong> brought the NextGen Tax Excellence Award and the
inaugural International Fiscal Association Travelling Lectureship Programme,
which drew over 200 professionals to engage with cutting-edge tax developments.</p>
<p><strong>May</strong> featured our International Tax Disputes Day Conference,
uniting local and overseas professionals on critical cross-border issues.
We also launched the TA Alumni Network and signed an MOU with Tax Executives
Institute.</p>
<p><strong>June</strong> expanded our international presence through a strategic
partnership with ICAEW, fostering collaboration between Singapore and UK
tax professionals.</p>
<p><strong>July</strong> deepened our Asia-Pacific connections through engagements
with CPA Australia Hong Kong and prominent Hong Kong tax academics.</p>
<p><strong>August</strong> launched the STARI-SG Singapore Tax Academy Research
Initiative, connecting academic research with real-world tax practice.</p>
<p><strong>September</strong> energised our community with the Young IFA Network
(YIN) - Singapore launch, creating a dynamic platform for exchange among
young tax professionals.</p>
<p><strong>October</strong> took us to the IFA Congress in Lisbon, where we
engaged with global tax leaders and showcased Singapore's expertise on
the international stage.</p>
<p><strong>November</strong> introduced our BEPS programme, preparing professionals
for the evolving landscape of international taxation.</p>
<p><strong>December</strong> concluded the year by celebrating our trainers,
course advisors, and examiners at the Tax Academy Appreciation event, and
partnering with the Law Society of Singapore to enhance tax education for
legal professionals.</p>
