<h3>2025: A Year of Growth, Connection, and Excellence at Tax Academy</h3>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

<p><strong>January</strong> launched our innovative e-learning platform for
Level 1 Income Tax, GST, and International Tax programmes, complete with
revision workshops that make quality tax education accessible across borders.
We kicked off the year by celebrating graduates at the Advanced Tax Programme
and Advanced GST Programme Graduation Event for the 2023 intakes.</p>
<p><strong>February</strong> strengthened our international partnerships by
welcoming the 10th cohort of the WU-TA Advanced Transfer Pricing Programme,
marking a decade of successful collaboration with WU Transfer Pricing Center.
We showcased our programme offerings at the SeamlessFilingFromSoftware
(#SFFS) exhibition at Revenue House and successfully concluded our comprehensive
6-day Leiden-TA Advanced Tax Treaties Programme.</p>
<p><strong>March</strong> marked a significant milestone as we formalised
our relationship with the International Fiscal Association through an MOU
signing, amplifying Singapore's voice in global tax conversations. We introduced
LEAP (Learning, Empowerment, Achievement, Progress) and signed a landmark
bursary agreement with NUS. We participated in the Deloitte Singapore Budget
and Tax Conference and had an enriching learning visit to EY Singapore.</p>
<p><strong>April</strong> brought exciting developments with the NextGen Tax
Excellence Award and the inaugural International Fiscal Association Travelling
Lectureship Programme, which attracted over 200 professionals eager to
engage with cutting-edge tax developments. We signed a bursary agreement
with NTU, conducted an IRAS site visit with HRC for NUS students, and participated
in the IFA Asia Pacific Conference in Kuala Lumpur.</p>
<p><strong>May</strong> featured our impactful International Tax Disputes
Day Conference, bringing together local and overseas professionals to address
critical cross-border issues. We also launched the TA Alumni Network and
strengthened industry connections through an MOU with Tax Executives Institute.</p>
<p><strong>June</strong> expanded our international footprint through a strategic
partnership with ICAEW, fostering valuable collaboration between Singapore
and UK tax professionals. We celebrated our community by hosting the inaugural
TA Alumni Network welcome event.</p>
<p><strong>July</strong> deepened our Asia-Pacific connections through meaningful
engagements with CPA Australia Hong Kong and prominent Hong Kong tax academics.
We also participated in the Catholic Junior College Accountancy Career
Fair alongside Accounting and Corporate Regulatory Authority (ACRA), Institute
of Singapore Chartered Accountants (ISCA), and Institute of Internal Auditors
Singapore (IIA)</p>
<p><strong>August</strong> launched the Singapore Tax Academy Research Initiative
(STARI), bridging academic research with real-world tax practice. We proudly
supported eight students from NTU and NUS in the TEI international tax
student case competition in Montreal, hosted the NextGen Award ceremony,
participated in ACCtraordinary 2025 at Ngee Ann Polytechnic, and conducted
our inaugural AI &amp; Tax Training powered by PwC Singapore.</p>
<p><strong>September</strong> energised our community with the Young IFA Network
(YIN) - Singapore launch, creating a dynamic platform for knowledge exchange
among emerging tax professionals. As a founding partner, we supported the
PS Centre in Ho Chi Minh City, participated in the ISCA AccountanCity event,
and attended alumni nights hosted by both NUS Tax Society and SMU Tax Society.</p>
<p><strong>October</strong> took us to the IFA Congress in Lisbon, where we
engaged with global tax leaders and showcased Singapore's expertise on
the international stage. We visited the first PS Centre in Shanghai Hongqiao
and reconnected with PS Centre Alliance partners.</p>
<p><strong>November</strong> introduced our comprehensive BEPS programme,
equipping professionals with essential knowledge for navigating the evolving
landscape of international taxation.</p>
<p><strong>December</strong> concluded the year on a high note by celebrating
our dedicated trainers, course advisors, and examiners at the Tax Academy
Appreciation Event, and forging a new partnership with the Law Society
of Singapore to enhance tax education for legal professionals.</p>
