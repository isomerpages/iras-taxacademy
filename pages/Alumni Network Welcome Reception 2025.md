<h3><strong>Tax Academy Alumni Network – Welcome Reception 2025</strong></h3>
<p>On 30 May 2025, the Tax Academy of Singapore (TA) hosted a Welcome Reception
to mark the official launch of its exclusive, invite-only Alumni Network.</p>
<p>The event brought together 200 alumni members, comprising graduates and
trainers from Tax Academy’s Level 3 Income Tax and GST programmes. The
Alumni Network aims to foster a close-knit community and create a space
where tax professionals can connect, collaborate, and engage in meaningful
discussions on tax-related matters.</p>
