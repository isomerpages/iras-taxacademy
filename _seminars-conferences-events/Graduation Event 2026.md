<h3><strong>Tax Academy Graduation Event &amp; NextGen Award Ceremony 2026</strong></h3>
<p>We hosted our Graduation Event &amp; NextGen Award Ceremony 2026 on 6
February 2026 at Civil Service Club @ Tessensohn. This year, we celebrated
approximately 90 participants who successfully graduated from the Advanced
Tax Programme (ATP) - Intake 1 &amp; 2/2024.&nbsp;</p>
<p>ATP is one of our flagship programmes, and completing this comprehensive
course represents a remarkable achievement. Our dedicated participants
completed 9 core modules and 2 electives over 150 hours of intensive training
â€“ a true testament to their commitment and perseverance. We extend our
heartfelt gratitude to all our ATP trainers for their invaluable expertise
in shaping these future tax professionals.</p>
<p>The ceremony also recognised our NextGen awardees for 2026. This prestigious
award embodies our commitment to nurturing the next generation of tax professionals
through early exposure to taxation and professional networking opportunities.
Award recipients gain exclusive access to Tax Academy's events and networking
sessions with industry experts, opening doors to meaningful career development.</p>
<p>Heartfelt congratulations to all our graduates and Merit Book Prize winners!
A special thank you to our wonderful community of attendees who made our
Graduation Event &amp; NextGen Award Ceremony such a meaningful and joyous
celebration.</p>
